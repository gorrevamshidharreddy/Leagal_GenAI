GenAI Legal Assistant for Indian SMEs
Problem Statement
Small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in India often struggle to understand complex legal contracts, identify hidden risks, and ensure compliance with Indian laws without expensive legal consultation.
Solution Overview
This project is a GenAI-powered legal assistant that helps Indian SMEs analyze business contracts and receive plain-language explanations, risk insights, and compliance warnings. The platform focuses on explainability, privacy, and practical decision support for non-legal users.

Key Features
-  Upload contracts (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
-  Contract type classification (Employment, Vendor, Service, Lease, etc.)
-  Clause & sub-clause extraction
-  Clause-level risk scoring (Low / Medium / High)
-  Indian legal compliance flagging
-  Ambiguity detection in contract language
-  Clause similarity matching with standard templates
-  SME-friendly contract templates
-  Overall contract risk assessment
-  Downloadable legal review PDF
-  Privacy-first local processing with audit logs

 Tech Stack
- Frontend: Streamlit  
- Backend & NLP: Python  
- Document Parsing: pdfplumber, python-docx  
- PDF Export: FPDF  
- Deployment: Streamlit Cloud
